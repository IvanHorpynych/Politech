Groups
 
Groups - logical associations between similar or disparate targets - allow you to efficiently manage targets as a single unit. The Groups folder contains any groups to which you have access, whether defined by you or by others. You may choose to:
Add targets to a group.
Associate a background image with a group to represent geographic or organizational relationships.
Include a subgroup within a group to depict hierarchical targets.
Monitor the global enterprise through a graphical display of groups.
Create events and jobs against a group to reduce repetitiveness.
View a report on all targets contained in a group.
