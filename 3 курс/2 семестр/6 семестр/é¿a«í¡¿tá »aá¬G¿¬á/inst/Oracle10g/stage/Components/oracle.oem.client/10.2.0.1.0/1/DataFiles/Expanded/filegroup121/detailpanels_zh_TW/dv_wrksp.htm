Workspace Management
 
A workspace is a virtual environment that one or more users can share to make changes to the data in the database. Workspace management involves managing one or more workspaces that can be shared by many users.

Use Workspace Manager to:
Enable and disable table versioning.
Create, edit, and remove workspaces.
Rollback or refresh a child workspace.
See differences between two workspaces, or differences between two savepoints in a workspace.
View and resolve conflicts between a child workspace and its parent.
Assign or revoke a user's Workspace Manager privileges.
